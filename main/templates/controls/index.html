Why?

Whether it be living at the University or surviving your first Hackathon, there is always a fundamental need to be healthier. That's where Boulder comes in, a highly-Pebble-based application that captures the motion from your Pebble watch to compare it to a data-set that was trained using a Machine Learning algorithm called a Support Vector Machine. Capturing these motions and associating them with learned sets allows us to perceive what exercises a user is working on and promptly reward them. An engaging experience for everyone!

How?

We reward users for exercising by providing them a highly engaging social experience on the website. Being able to securely add/remove users with a pending system and a search functionality that only finds exact email queries, the common user doesn't have to worry about being stalked/spied on/invaded or any of the other typical horror story motifs. Users can see their most recent activities, view suggestions of healthy activities from the admins, and even see their friends activities on the newsfeed.
